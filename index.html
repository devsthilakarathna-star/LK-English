About LK English

LK English is a versatile language tool designed for the Sri Lankan community. Our goal is to make it easy to translate text and find definitions in Sinhala, Tamil, and English. We believe that technology can help bridge language barriers and connect people.

This project was created and developed by Shehan Thilakarathna, a passionate front-end developer dedicated to building practical and user-friendly web applications.
